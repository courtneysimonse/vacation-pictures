Steelhead Ironman 70.3 2019 Trip

A great trip!

Bill and Rose's trip to Michigan for the Steelhead Ironman 70.3 race.

We traveled to cousin Samatha and Todd's house on Friday and it was a weekend of gracious hospitality! We made a new friend in Pungo and Samatha fixed some amazing plant-based meals to carbo load for the race!

On Saturday we traveled to Benton Harbor so I could check in and we met up with MJ and his crew doing some pre-race day training!

Got all checked in and then we had lunch with MJ, Steve and their friends.

We enjoyed the restaurant and visting with the gang!

Saturday afternoon back to Samantha's and some rest, more time with our friend Pungo :) and more carbo loading.

Todd took us on a tour of Kalamazoo and we toured Western Michigan University.

We also saw where Samantha work's now and also where Todd works. It was a nice evening for a ride!

Sunday was race day with an early start.

We were all ready and conditions were great with Lake Michigan very calm, and no wind on the bike!

Rose and Samantha were the camera crew and Samantha had zoom lense camera that took amazing pics!

The racers really appreciated Rose, Samantha, and Todd's cheering!

Bill improved his time by 33 minutes from last year! MJ placed 2nd in his age group, and Steve placed 8th in his age group

Everyone had a great race! Plans are already being made to meet in Traverse City, MI next summer for that Ironman event!

Sunday after the race we traveled to Terra Haute, In., and spent the night there. Rose visited with here friends from college Mandy and Ger. Bill got in the hot tub.

Monday we headed home, but along the way we made a stop in Vincennes, Indiana, and had a nice visit with Mike and Deb Ross.

We had breakfast at Dot's and then toured the Vincennes Riverwalk.

We also went by Rose's college dorm at Vincennes - great memories!
