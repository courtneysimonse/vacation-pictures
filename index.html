Sicily Trip 2019

A great trip!

We started our trip at the Atlanta airport. Mom was looking stylish in her glasses. We were all excited and ready to go!

We had a layover at Heathrow airport in London. They had some different security protocols.

We stayed at Geneva our first and last nights of the trip. The restaurant across the street from our Airbnb was great!

Finally made it to the Mother Country!

We stayed on the island of Ortigia, the old town in Syracuse.

Our ill-fated trip to the Baroque hill-town of Noto left Nancy and I carsick!

Mom and Connie Sue went on a hike up Mt. Etna.

We wandered around Augusta looking for landmarks attached to our roots.
